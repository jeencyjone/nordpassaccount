Advanced Security for Your Digital Life

NordPass employs a zero-knowledge architecture, meaning your data is encrypted and decrypted locally on your device. We never have access to your master password or the contents of your vault. This approach ensures that only you can access your sensitive information.

Our security model includes multiple layers of protection, from biometric authentication options to two-factor authentication. Each security measure is designed to work seamlessly while providing robust defense against unauthorized access attempts.

With NordPass, you benefit from continuous security monitoring and regular independent audits. Our commitment to transparency means we openly share our security practices and undergo rigorous testing to maintain the highest standards of protection for our users.
